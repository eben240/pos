ABOUT



Possibility Church is established by a command from Heaven.We are ONE body in places to propagate the gospel of christ in effective teaching, and worshipping GOD in the fullness of his praises(Mark 16:15). We desire to be a path for the lost, a home for the rejected, a great people, generous and unified.Our aim is making the world know the only way to life and the father is JESUS.

ESTHER
PETER

Senior Pastor

Esther Peter serves as Senior Pastor of Possibility Church.

She is passionate about seeing the people transformed by the Word

of God and making heaven more crowded! Esther is the author of Transit,

a book dedicated to taking people closer to God. In it he shares his

personal journey in growing closer to God, uncovers disciplines that were

critical to that journey, and invites each of us to ignite an authentic

relationship with God. He and his wife Jill have three active sons—Ethan,

Austin, and Ashton—so when they’re not at church, they’re usually at a sports game!
